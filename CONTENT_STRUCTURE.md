# Eduktok Content Structure Guide
## 30 Units × 15 Lessons = 450 Total Lessons

This document outlines the complete curriculum structure for Eduktok, following a progressive language learning approach similar to Rosetta Stone.

---

## 📚 BEGINNER LEVEL (Units 1-10)

### Unit 1: Basic Colors (15 lessons)
**Goal**: Recognize and name primary and secondary colors

1. **Listening**: Red objects (apple, car, flower)
2. **Speaking**: Pronouncing "red"
3. **Listening**: Blue objects (sky, water, shirt)
4. **Speaking**: Pronouncing "blue"
5. **Listening**: Yellow objects (sun, banana, taxi)
6. **Speaking**: Pronouncing "yellow"
7. **Listening**: Green objects (grass, tree, frog)
8. **Speaking**: Pronouncing "green"
9. **ListeningFour**: Mixed color identification
10. **Speaking**: Color combinations
11. **Listening**: Orange objects (orange fruit, sunset)
12. **Listening**: Purple objects (grape, flower)
13. **Speaking**: All colors review
14. **ListeningFour**: Advanced color matching
15. **Speaking**: Describing colored objects

**Images needed**: ~45 (3 per lesson × 15 lessons)

## 🎯 KEY PRINCIPLES

1. **Image-Text Coherence**: Every image MUST accurately represent the text
2. **Progressive Difficulty**: Each unit builds on previous knowledge
3. **Variety**: Mix lesson types to maintain engagement
4. **Cultural Sensitivity**: Use diverse, inclusive imagery
5. **Clarity**: Images should be unambiguous and clear
6. **Consistency**: Maintain visual style across all units
